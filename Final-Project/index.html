Doodle Jump Game!

Jump as high as you can and avoid the obstacles to get a HIGH-SCORE!

How to play

Use the green platforms to bounce your way up. Be sure to avoid the obstacles that will start to fall from the sky because if you get hit you'll lose the game and have to restart!
