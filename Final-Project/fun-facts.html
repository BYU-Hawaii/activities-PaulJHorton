Fun Facts About Jumping

  • The highest recorded jump by a human is over 8 feet!
  • Frogs are known for their incredible jumping abilities, with some species able to leap over 20 times their body length.
  • Kangaroos can jump up to 10 feet high and cover a distance of 30 feet in a single leap.
